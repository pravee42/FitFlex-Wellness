Popular Diets Explained

Mediterranean Diet

Focuses on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, olive oil, fish, and nuts.

Keto Diet

High-fat, low-carb diet aimed at achieving ketosis for fat burning.

Vegetarian Diet

Excludes meat but includes plant-based foods like fruits, vegetables, grains, and nuts.

Paleo Diet

Emphasizes foods that would have been available to our Paleolithic ancestors, such as lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds.
