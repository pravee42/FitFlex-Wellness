Top 10 Exercises for Your Fitness

Get fit with our top exercises that suit all levels.

Push-Ups

Strengthen your chest, shoulders, and triceps with this classic exercise.

Squats

Build your legs and glutes with this essential lower body move.

Burpees

Boost your cardiovascular fitness with this full-body exercise.

Plank

Enhance your core strength and stability with the plank.

Lunges

Improve your balance and leg strength with lunges.

Jumping Jacks

Get your heart pumping and improve coordination with jumping jacks.

Dumbbell Rows

Strengthen your back and arms with dumbbell rows.

High Knees

Boost your cardio with high knees, an effective high-intensity move.

Leg Raises

Focus on your lower abs with leg raises for a stronger core.
